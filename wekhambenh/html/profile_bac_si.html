Dr. Sonia Kabwa

Dr. Sonia Kabwa is a family specialist doctor with more than 20 years of experience. Her work is characterized by a deep understanding of family dynamics, compassionate care, and a commitment to improving the health and well-being of her patients.

Dr. Kabwa believes in a holistic approach to medicine, addressing not only physical health but also emotional and social aspects. She is passionate about promoting preventive healthcare and empowering families to make informed decisions about their health.
